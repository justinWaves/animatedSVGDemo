Welcome

to the Evolution Music Family

Yes, we are truly a family here at Evolution; Not only because we are founded and run by an actual family, but because we believe in family values within our company. Throughout the years, we have experienced just about every corner of the entertainment business, so we strive to only have the best parts of that be a part of Evolution. We hope that each day you come to work, you are passionate about what you do, and have people around you who are just as enthusiastic as you are. They say, “When you love what you do, you won’t work a day in your life”. We are also fortunate to have expanded our Evolution family to reach over 4 continents so far. We are excited for the future, to continue furthering our reach, and we look forward to finding even more talented & exceptional individuals to join us along the way. We hope to help our planet become a happier place and to bring even more great music, events, instruments, films and more across borders and around the world.
